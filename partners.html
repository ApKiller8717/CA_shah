CA Jwalin Shah, Partner

B.com, FCA, Founder Partner

About Me

Jwalin Shah is a Chartered Accountant and the Founder of Shah Panchal and Associates, based in Ahmedabad. A Fellow member of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India, he brings over six years of rich and diverse experience. He has deep expertise in Income Tax, GST, RERA, Corporate Taxation, Auditing, Assurance, and Corporate Compliance. He holds a Bachelor’s degree in Commerce from the University of Gujarat and is known for delivering cost-efficient, professional services. Throughout his career, he has resolved complex tax controversies, represented clients before tax authorities, and guided them through GST and corporate legal structures. His work also includes consultancy for company formation, intellectual property rights, and advisory for overseas entities and NRIs. Jwalin has contributed significantly to framing financial strategies and roadmaps for Indian start-ups and foreign entrants. Recognized for his innovative ideas and strategic thinking, he is widely respected in his field. A future-oriented leader, he focuses on empowering the next generation through skill development and livelihood initiatives as part of his commitment to social responsibility.

About Me

I am an FCA currently pursuing degrees in commerce and law, with over 10 years of comprehensive experience in delivering high-impact consulting across law, taxation, finance, revenue, strategy, and bespoke business solutions for diverse industries and dynamic entrepreneurs. At D. R. Panchal & Co., I handled complex assignments involving strategic business location assessments, legal-technical land verification, due diligence, compliance structuring, and the formulation and execution of business strategies. My mission is to empower clients to establish, refine, and scale their businesses by removing constraints, enhancing operational clarity, and unlocking new pathways for sustainable growth.
